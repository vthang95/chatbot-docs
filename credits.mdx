Credits are a way to pay for your API calls.

### How to get credits

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to [Settings](https://platform.minostack.com/settings) and click on `Buy Credits` Button.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ![Buy Credits](/images/buy_credits.png)
    A modal will appear, where you can enter the desired credit amount and then click the `Buy` button.
    <Note>
      A minimum of 10,000 credits is required to topup your credits.
      1 credit is equivalent to 1 VND.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    A QR code will be generated. Please scan it and complete the payment for the amount displayed in your banking application.

    <Note>
      - Do not close the QR Code window until you have successfully purchased the credits.
      - Do not change the money amount or transaction description.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step>
  Once the purchase is completed, an order will be created and your credit balance will be updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>
